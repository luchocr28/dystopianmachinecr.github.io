melancholy melodies, gothic atmosphere, and mechanical beats

Based in San Jose, Costa Rica, this gothic metal project blends haunting melodies with electronic elements to create an immersive soundscape that captivates the listener. With a focus on melancholic themes, the project combines heavy guitar riffs and atmospheric synths.

Influences: The 69 Eyes, Nine Inch Nails, Paradise Lost, Depeche Mode, Rammstein, Katatonia, Tool, Placebo, Type O Negative & Anathema.
